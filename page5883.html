Workbench Description

Note: The image below shows the location of the common toolbars.  Because 3D Simulation for Manufacturing does not have a workbench of its own, the image shows the DPM - Assembly Process Simulation workbench.  Consequently, you may see commands on the image below that are not part of your configuration; the image is provided to show the common commands.

The 3D Manufacturing Simulation product provides toolbars that are found throughout the DELMIA product line.  

One feature of the DELMIA infrastructure is that the initial location of the toolbars is standardized: 3D Simulation for Manufacturing toolbars appear by default on the top toolbar docking area; process planning toolbars appear by default on the left toolbar docking area; common V5 toolbars appear on the bottom toolbar docking area, and toolbars for a specific application (e.g., DPM Assembly Process Simulation) on the right toolbar docking area.
