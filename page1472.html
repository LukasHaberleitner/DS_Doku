Zooming In

This task explains how to zoom in by predetermined increments.

  • Select View > Modify > Zoom In, or click Zoom In in the View toolbar.

    You zoom in by one increment each time you click on the icon. To zoom up close (as illustrated), click on the icon several times in succession.
    Before After

You can also select View > Zoom In Out and drag (left mouse button) to zoom in progressively, not by increments.
