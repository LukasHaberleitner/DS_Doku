Hyperlinks and Process Engineer Attachments

Attachments on processes, products, resources, and welds defined in Process Engineer are automatically mapped to DPM hyperlinks when opened in DPM applications.

This mapping is bi-directional, which means that attachments that have been defined in Process Engineer can be modified or deleted from DPM and saved back to the Manufacturing Hub.
