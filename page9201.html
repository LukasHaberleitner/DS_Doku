CatDxfExportVersionEnum (Enumeration)

enum CatDxfExportVersionEnum {
  catDxfExportVersionEnumR12,
  catDxfExportVersionEnumR13,
  catDxfExportVersionEnumR14,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2000,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2004,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2007,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2010,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2013,
  catDxfExportVersionEnum2018
}
Available values of the DXF export version option.
See also:
Values:
R12
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD R12.
R13
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD R13.
R14
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD R14.
2000
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2000 (same as 2002).
2004
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2004 (same as 2005 and 2006).
2007
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2007 (same as 2008).
2010
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2010 (same as 2010).
2013
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2013 (same as 2015).
2018
Export a DXF or DWG with a format AutoCAD 2018 (same as 2019 and 2020).
