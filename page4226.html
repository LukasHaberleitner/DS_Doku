Positioning a Group or Activity within the Process

This procedure describes how to move an activity or a group of activities from one place within the process to another. The activities may be inspection activities, such as Define a Point, or simulation activities, such as Create a Viewpoint.

  1. Select the group you want to reposition.

    You can either select a group command, as you see below, or multi-select several sequential activities.

  2. Using the right mouse button, select Group - Group1 object > Move After.

  3. Select the activity after which you wish to position the group.

    The group is repositioned within the process.
