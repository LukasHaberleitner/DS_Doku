Working with a Part and a Product

This task describes the methodology for working with a Product and a Part.
 

Updating an Assembly/Assemblies and all Parts in the Database

  1. Apply the Overwrite rule to the Product.

  2. Apply the Overwrite rule to the Parts.

  3. Save the documents in the database.

Updating an Assembly/Assemblies and Some of the Parts in the Database

  1. Apply the Overwrite rule to the Product.

  2. Apply the Overwrite /Reload (to keep the Parts contained in the database) rule to the Parts.

  3. Save the documents in the database.
