Breaking a Route

This task shows you how to break a route.
1. Click the Break Route button .
2. Click the route you want to break at the point where you want it broken. The route will be broken and a connector symbol will appear. The route has been broken into two and you can move it if needed, as shown in the image below.

If the Snap To Point feature is turned on, the line will break at the grid line that is closest to the point where you clicked.
