Creating and Moving an Action    

This task explains how to create and move an action. 

  1. Open the CATSystem created in Creating a Graph from a Sample.

  2. Click the icon, then select the subject component (Medicine for example or its renamed version), and click the Tissue object. The Action.1 is added to the subject component. Rename this action to "saturates".

  1. Repeat the step above for the other objects contained in the CATSystem file. The result should be identical to the graphic opposite.
To move an action as a whole element and detach it completely from its initial subject and object, select the arrow representing the action right in the middle then drag it. To move either extremity of an action, select the green spot at the extremity of the action that you want to move and drag it to the component to be attached.
