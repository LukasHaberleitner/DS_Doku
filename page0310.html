Terminating a Job

You can use one of the following methods to terminate a job within Abaqus for CATIA V5:

  • In the Job Manager, select a job from the list, and click Kill.

  • In the Job Monitor dialog box for a particular job, click Kill. (For more information, see Monitoring the Progress of a Job.)

Abaqus for CATIA V5 asks you for confirmation, terminates the job, and updates the status of the job in the Job Manager to Terminated.
