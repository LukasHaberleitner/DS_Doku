Inviting More People to the Current 3D Conference

 

You can invite people to a 3D Conference even after the conference has begun.

 

  1. Right-click the current 3D Conference and select Invite others to the current 3D conference from the contextual menu.
    The Invite others to a meeting dialog box appears. You invite more people in the same manner as you invited the original invitees. See Creating a 3D Conference.
