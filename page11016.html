Optimal CATIA PLM Usability for Knowledge Advisor  

When working with ENOVIA V5 VPM, the safe save mode ensures that you only create data in CATIA that can be correctly saved in ENOVIA LCA.

ENOVIA V5 VPM offers two different storage modes: Workpackage (Document kept - Publications Exposed) and Explode (Document not kept).
