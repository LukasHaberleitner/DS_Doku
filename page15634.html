About Technological Packages

  • A technological package is always linked to a product structure.
  • A technological package can be loaded only when the product structure it belongs to is already loaded.
  • A technological package cannot be configured outside a product.
  • No VPMV4 or Smart Team integration is provided.
  • No support of objects or attributes exposition is provided.
  • Be cautious when working with technological packages that have the same name: If you create a relation based on 2 reals that have the same name, the relation will detect a loop.
