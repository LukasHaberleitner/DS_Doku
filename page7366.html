About Annotation Body Placement During Annotation Creation

You can place the annotation body at a particular location before validating the annotation creation.
You can control the way an annotation with leader is created. You can decide the sequence of how an annotation with leader is created. This is helpful in order to place the annotation body at a particular location during its creation itself.

There is a preference Allow to indicate annotation body position in Tools > Options> Mechanical Design> Functional Tolerancing & Annotation >Annotation> Annotation. When this option is checked, the sequence of annotation creation is as follows:

1. Launch the annotation creation command,
2. Select the position to place the annotation leader (the panel is not launched yet),
3. Place the annotation body at particular location (annotation body will follow the mouse cursor until it is placed). As soon as the annotation body is placed, the annotation creation panel is displayed,
4. You can enrich the surface selection by selecting different additional surfaces or enter text in text field,
5. Validate the changes and annotation creation by clicking in empty space or through panel.

If this option is not chcked, the sequence of annotation creation is as follows:
1. Launch the annotation creation command,
2. Select the position to place the annotation leader (the panel is launched immediately),
3. You can enrich the surface selection by selecting different additional surfaces or insert text in text field of panel. At the same time, by pressing ‘Ctrl’ key on keyboard, annotation body will follow the cursor,
4. Validate the changes and annotation creation by clicking in empty space or through panel.
