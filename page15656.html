Optimal CATIA PLM Usability for Product Knowledge Template

When working with ENOVIA V5 VPM, the safe save mode ensures that you only create data in CATIA that can be correctly saved in ENOVIA V5 VPM.

ENOVIA V5 VPM offers two different storage modes: Workpackage (Document kept - Publications Exposed) and Explode (Document not kept). Product Knowledge Template (PKT) has been configured to work in the Workpackage mode.
