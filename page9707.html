Sub GetLengthInMetre( dLength, bCurrManikinPose)
This gets the auto walk length in metres
Parameters:
bCurrManikinPosition
The bCurrManikinPosition flag is used to obtain the walk length for autowalk dynamically based on the location of manikin irrespective of its previous MoveToPosture activity; in a line tracking scenario if the manikin has a body constraint with a moving object then, the Degree Of Freedom (DOF)of previous MoveToPosture activity gives only the static walk length and is incorrect. By defuault, this value will be taken as FALSE
Returns:
dLength walk length in metre
