Connecting Routables

This task shows you how to connect a routable to another routable. Routables are path reservations, space reservation, boundaries, or runs.

There are two kinds of connections: branch and end-to-end. In a branch connection the end of one element connects to the middle of another. In an end-to-end connection two ends are connected together. In the examples below the two path reservations  will be connected to each other, first as a branch and then as an end-to-end connection.
When you connect two elements one becomes the Follower and the other becomes the Leader. In the example above, if you make the run on the right  the Follower and the one on the left the Leader, after you connect them, the Follower will follow the Leader to maintain the connection.
1. Place the mouse pointer over the element you want to designate the Follower and click the right mouse button.
2. From the pull-down menu that displays, select the element you want to modify, in this case Path Reservation, and select the Connect Routes option. (Alternatively, you can click Edit, the element, in this case Path Reservation, and select Connect Routes.)
3. Select the Leader Connector. To select, move your mouse pointer over the path reservation you wish to designate the Leader and click on either the support or center line connector. See Information below.
When you move the mouse pointer over the routable you will see two lines alternatively, as you move the pointer from bottom to top. The solid line is the support line connector, and the dashed line is the center line connector (illustrated below). The second routable will connect to whichever line you select. Select the line by clicking.
4. Select which end of the Follower you want to connect to the Leader.  The Follower will connect with the Leader after you click.
The two elements will connect at the point where the two support or center lines would intersect. If you want to change the position, see Moving Nodes.
5. To make an end-to-end connection replace Step 3 above with the following:

Select the Leader Connector. To select, move your mouse pointer over the path reservation you wish to designate the Leader and click on the arrow that displays at whichever end you want to connect. As you move the pointer up and down the arrow will move, depending on whether the pointer is over the support or center line connector, as explained above.
As an alternative, connect the routable as follows: Click the Connect button and select one of the elements you want to connect. The element selected first will be the follower and will move to join the leader element. You must click on one of the connector indicators on the follower run. Select the leader element. The first run will move to connect to the leader and the connection symbol will display.
