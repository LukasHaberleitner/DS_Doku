Toolbars

 

 

This section describes the various s available in the Quick Surface Reconstruction workbench.
Other menus are documented in the Infrastructure User's Guide.

The toolbars

  • Cloud Edition
  • Scan Creation
  • Curve Creation
  • Domain Creation
  • Surface Creation
  • Operations
  • Transformations
  • Segmentation
  • Analysis
  • Deviation Check
  • Wireframe
are located on the right in the default set-up.

The View Management tool bar is located at the bottom of the screen.
