Computing the Boundaries of the Flange

This task shows you how to analyze a surface and automatically compute the boundaries of the flange. Automatic Flange Boundaries is particularly useful when many simple flanges are designed on the part.
Open ProcessPositioning_InitP1.CATPart from the samples directory.


  1. Click Automatic Flange Boundaries in the Process Part Separation toolbar.

  2. Select a surface and click Apply.
    Fillet bones are detected.
    They are fillets that go from one side of the part to another without ambiguity.
    For example, such as the orange area shown below.

    An Automatic Flange Boundaries feature is created.
    It contains the Flange Boundaries curves.

    Use this result as input in Stamp Partition.
    The Flange Boundaries curves split the part automatically to define the flanges.
    In complex cases like this

    automatic detection is impossible. Define the flange manually.
