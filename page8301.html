Flipping a Part

This task shows you how to flip a hanger. "Flipping" here means connecting the hanger by using a different connector than the one currently used.

1. Click the Flip Part Position button and then click on the hanger you want to flip. You can repeat this step to keep trying out all the connectors on the hanger.

In the image below, the hanger has two connectors. This step has been used to try out both connectors.

Some hangers cannot be flipped. In such cases, you will get an error message.
