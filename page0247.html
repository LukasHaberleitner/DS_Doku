Using Submodeling

This section describes how you can use submodeling to study a local part of a model with a refined mesh based on interpolation of the solution from a global model with a relatively coarse mesh. Submodeling is most useful when you want to obtain an accurate, detailed solution in a local region and the detailed modeling of that local region has negligible effect on the overall solution. Submodeling is described in detail in About submodeling in the Abaqus Analysis Guide.
