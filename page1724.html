Instantiating Behaviors

To instantiate behaviors:

  1.  
 Select the type with which you want to associate a behavior in BKT.
  1.  
 Click one of the behaviors icon. The behavior is displayed in the Behaviors View.
  1.  
 To see behaviors, double-click the behavior in the Behaviors View. The Behaviors tab is now available.
  1.  
 Double-click the type node displayed in the Behaviors tab to expand it and click one of the behaviors icon. The behavior is displayed below the object name.
