Opening Workshop Documents  

After workshop documents have been extracted (either individually or with the batch extraction command), Open Workshop Document allows you to view a list of all documents associated with a selected product or operation and quickly open any document in the list.

Before using Open Workshop Document, documents must have already been extracted for the selected product or operation.

Opening a Workshop Document
 

  1. Select Open Workshop Document .

  2. Select a product in the 3D viewer. The Open Workshop Documents dialog box lists all of the workshop documents associated with the selected product:

    Note: Alternatively, activities or operations may also be selected from the PPR tree instead of the 3D viewer.

  3. Select a workshop document from the list, and click Open to view it:

    XML documents appear in within browsers.
