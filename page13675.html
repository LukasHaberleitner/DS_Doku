Working with ENOVIA LCA: Optimal CATIA PLM Usability

When working with ENOVIA LCA, a new mode ensures that you only create data in CATIA that can be correctly saved in ENOVIA.

ENOVIA LCA offers two different storage modes: Workpackage (Document kept - Publications Exposed) and Explode (Document not kept). The Workpackage mode is the recommended mode.

The safe save mode means that, when working with ENOVIA LCA, certain commands (Small Assemblies and Cutback) are unavailable (i.e. grayed) on entering the workbench.

Cutback and Small Assemblies commands have been configured to work in the Workpackage mode only.
Note: you cannot place small assemblies between two different workpackages.
