Customizing

Before you start your first working session, you can customize the way you work to suit your habits. This type of customization is stored in permanent setting files: these settings will not be lost if you end your session.

  1. Select Tools > Options.
    The Options dialog box opens.

  2. Select the Equipment & Systems category in the left-hand box.

  3. Click the Circuit Board Design workbench.

    The Graphic and Components tabs appear:

    The Graphic tab lets you define the constraint area color.

    The Components tab lets you define a catalog and other parameters.

  4. Set options in these tabs according to your needs.

  5. Click OK when done.
