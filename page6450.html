Using Electrical 3D Design
and Documentation

This task explains how to set up the environment to work with CATIA - Electrical 3D Design & Documentation.
CATIA - Electrical 3D Design & Documentation includes two workbenches:

Electrical 3D Design Assembly workbench to work at the level of an assembly of electrical objects.
Electrical 3D Design Part workbench to add electrical behavior at the level of a part.
