Inserting a New Product

This task shows you how to insert a product in an existing assembly.
Open the ManagingComponents01.CATProduct document.
In the specification tree, select ManagingComponents01 and click the Product icon. A new product is created in the specification tree: Product2 (Product2.1) in our example.
If you want you can define the default part number of the product to be inserted. To see how this is done refer to Customizing Product Structure Settings.
