Visualizing Extrema

This task shows you how to visualize the extrema on an image.

The Von Mises Stress image must be activated, as seen in the previous task Generate an image.

  1. Click Image Extrema . 

    The Extrema Creation dialog box appears.

    To know more about global and local extrema, refer to Creating Extrema.

  2. Select the Global option check box.

  3. Enter 2 as Maximum extrema at most value.

  4. Click OK.
