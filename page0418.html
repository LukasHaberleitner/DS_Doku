SIMULIA Abaqus for CATIA V5 Complete Licensing

You can now set up licensing for both Abaqus for CATIA V5 (AFC) and Abaqus for CATIA V5 Prerequisite (ACP) by installing a single product that is newly available for this release, SIMULIA Abaqus for CATIA V5 Complete (ACC). ACP is no longer available as a license. You now have to license ACC, which includes licenses for all prerequisites and for AFC.
