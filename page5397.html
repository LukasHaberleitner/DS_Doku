Producibility and Flattening

Producibility commands provide data to help you predict the way the plies fibers are laid on the mold surface.
The fibers are displayed on the plies surface with respect to the chosen propagation type algorithm.
Once you have identified the areas with a potential problematic propagation, you can modify the initial composites elements parameters
or introduce new elements (such as Cut-Pieces) to correct the problems.

The final result of the Producibility for Hand Layup is the Flattening which represents the unfolded ply.
