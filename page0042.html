Displaying Coordinates Information In a Balloon

This task shows you how to display the coordinates of a fastener in a balloon with respect to the position of the fastener product.

Open the ABF_AllTypeFast_01.CATProduct document.

  1. Click BiW Fastener X, Y, Z Screen Report from the BiW Tools toolbar.

  2. Point out a fastener in the 3D geometry or in the specification tree.
