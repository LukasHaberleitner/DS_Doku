Editing Products within the Process

This procedure describes how to add data to product documents.

You can access the full functionality of all V5 workbenches available within your product configuration when you are working with DPM.  Therefore, this procedure will provide two examples of changes you can make to your product document while you are in APS. 
Remember that product documents can be used as either products or resources within your process document.  Therefore, you may edit the contents of either kind of product document.
