About Automatic Insert

Automatic insert enables you to create shots for a move activity without using Record (Insert) on the Recorder pop-up toolbar.
To use automatic insert, use Tools > Options > Digital Mockup > DMU Fitting > DMU Manipulation tab.
You have two configuration options for automatic insert:
  • On mouse release: Once you let go of the mouse as you move an object, the place where you let go of the mouse is recorded as a shot.
  • While mouse moving:
    • This option looks at the variables below to determine how often to insert shots.
    • Distance: Specifies the minimum distance step during simulation recording .
    • Angle: Specifies the maximum rotation angle step during simulation recording.
