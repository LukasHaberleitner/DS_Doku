Non-semantic and Semantic Usage

  The goal of the Functional Tolerancing and Annotation product is to fully cover the ISO or ASME/ANSI standards semantic and syntax definitions. Non-semantic 3D annotation can be used in case of Functional Tolerancing and Annotation lacking capabilities or company usage of symbols and syntax that are not covered by standards.
The following Semantic 3D Annotations are available:
  • Datum
  • Datum target
  • Toleranced dimensions applied to Features of Size:
    • Circle
    • Sphere
    • Cylinder
    • Tab/Slot
    • Angular Tab/Slot
    • Elongated Hole
    • Rectangular Holes
  • Toleranced dimensions applied to non-Features of Size:
    • Oriented dimension.
Note that semantic Toleranced dimensions cannot be faked.
