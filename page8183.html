Glossary

E
ergonomics

An applied science concerned with designing and arranging things people use so that the people and things interact most efficiently and safely.

M

maximum acceptable initial force

 The force required to put an object in motion.

maximum acceptable sustained force

 The force required to keep an object in motion.

maximum acceptable weight

 The weight that the selected population can carry with reasonable safety.

N
NIOSH

National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health

R
RULA

Rapid Upper Limb Assessment
RULA analysis Developed to investigate the exposure of individual workers to risks associated with work-related upper limb disorders.
