Creating a Frame and a Title Block

This task will show you how to insert a frame and a title block in the background of the sheet you defined.
If you do not want to have the specification tree displayed, press the F3 key.

  1. Click the drawing window to activate it.

  2. Select Edit > Sheet Background from the menu bar to switch to the background.

  3. Click Frame and Title Block in the Drawing toolbar.

    The Manage Frame and Title Block dialog box is displayed.

  4. Choose a style from the Style of title block drop-down list. For the purpose of this exercise, choose Drawing Titleblock Sample 1. You can notice that a preview of the frame and title block is displayed in the dialog box.

  5. Click the action you want to perform from the list of actions; in this case, click Create.

  6. Click OK. Wait while the frame and title block are being created.

  7. Select Edit > Working Views from the menu bar to switch back to the working views.
