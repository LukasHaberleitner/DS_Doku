Buckling Analysis

This chapter contains buckling analysis problems.
A buckling analysis consists in finding the buckling mode shapes and the buckling critical factors corresponding to a specified load case applied to an elastic structure.

You will find in this chapter the following tests:

Buckling of a Straight Beam (Out-of-Plane Buckling)
Buckling of a Straight Beam (In-Plane Buckling)
Lateral Buckling of Narrow Rectangular Beam
