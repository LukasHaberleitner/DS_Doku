Prismatic Machining Toolbars

The Prismatic Machining workbench includes specific icon toolbars:

  • Machining Operations

  • Machining Features

  • Probing Operations

  • Multi-Pockets Operations.

The other toolbars in the workbench are common to all the Machining products and are described in the NC Manufacturing Infrastructure User's Guide.
