Creating a Connect Surface

This task shows you how to create a Connect Surface to fill gaps remaining after the creation of extended surfaces.
Connect Surface is useful to fill small gaps. Large gaps, especially at corners, or with sharp edges, may not be filled.
Before you begin, see information in Creating an Addendum Design.
Open CleanContour01.CATPart from the samples directory.

  1. Click Connect Surface in the Part Extension toolbar.

  2. Select two Surface Extensions to connect.
    • Select the first edge or surface extension.
    • Select the second edge or surface extension.

    If you pick a Surface Extension, the planes are displayed for local modification.
    A Connect Surface is created between the two selections.

  3. Select the First Curve and the Second Curve.
    If the tension of the result is not satisfactory, or if the inputs are not Surface Extensions, expand the Parameters section.

  4. Select the Part Surface, that is the surface on which extended surfaces have been created leaving a gap.

  5. Specify continuity options
    • Part Continuity
    • Inner Connect Surface Continuity
    • Outer Connect Surface Continuity

    A Connect Surface feature is created. It contains the tension parameters and the generated geometry.
