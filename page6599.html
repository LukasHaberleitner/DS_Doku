Arranging Junctions

You can automatically arrange the breakout angles of branches at junctions according to an

  • Equal angle pattern, or an
  • Umbrella-like pattern.

Prefer the equal angle pattern for junctions located inside the harness and the umbrella-like pattern for junctions at the ends of harnesses.

A third command, User Pattern, lets you define your own branch arrangement rules. To do so, you must implement CAA V5 interface CATIEhfUIPArrangeJunction.
Important: Without CAA V5 development, this functionality is not available.
