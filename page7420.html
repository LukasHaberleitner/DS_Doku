Marking Non-semantic Annotations

This task shows you how to mark non-semantic annotations with a wavy red line in the specification tree and geometry.
It allows you to graphically distinguish non-semantic annotations (datum, datum targets, geometric tolerances, dimensions) from semantic one, see Concepts.
