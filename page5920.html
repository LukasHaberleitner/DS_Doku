User Tasks

The Generative Drafting workbench provides a simple method to create and modify views on a predefined sheet. You may also add, modify and/or delete dress-up and 2D elements to these views. All this is performed on a sheet which may include a frame and a title block and will eventually be printed.

 
Before you begin, we recommend you de-activate the Sketcher Grid in the Visualization toolbar.
