Getting Started

  This tutorial will guide you step-by-step through your first Tolerancing Review session, allowing you to get acquainted with the product. You just need to follow the instructions as you progress.
  The Getting Started section is composed of the following tasks:
  Setting the Options
Entering the DMU Tolerancing Review Workbench
Visualizing the Annotations
Visualizing the Related Surfaces
Filtering Tolerance Annotations
Publishing Dimensioning & Tolerancing Review
This scenario should take about 10 minutes to complete.
