Customizing for Human Task Simulation  

This page deals with Human Task Simulation customizing options.

The General tab has options to honor constraints and/or to update analyses during simulation. AutoWalk specifications may also be set in this tab.

The Cut Copy Paste tab allows you to define whether the first of last activity is placed at the current position or the compass position.

The Human Task Display tab allows you to customize the appearance of MoveToPosture, Walk, and AutoWalk activities.

The Joint Speed tab allows you to customize the speeds of the joints.
