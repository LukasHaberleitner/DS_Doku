Product Data Filtering in a Nutshell

Product Data Filtering helps you manage and protect intellectual property by providing a filtering mechanism integrated to your exchange processes. OEM, supplier chain and risk sharing partners are provided with only data they need to share to complete their product definition operations. As a result, your company knowledge is well kept without impairing data exchange requirement.

Before Reading this Guide

Prior to reading the Product Data Filtering User's Guide, you are recommended to have a look at the Infrastructure User's Guide for  information on the generic capabilities common to all products.
