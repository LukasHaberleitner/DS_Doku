Creating a Blend Between Surfaces

This task shows you how to blend two surfaces, that is create one surface connecting both initial surfaces, while specifying the continuity type.

  1. Click  the FreeStyle Blend Surface icon:

 

The Blend Surface dialog box is displayed.
 

  1. Select the edge of the initial surface closest to the second surface.
    The boundary is highlighted.

  2. Select the edge of the second surface closest to the initial surface.

 
 

A blend surface is automatically computed.
 

  1. Click OK in the Blend Surface dialog box to validate the blend surface.
 

End of the Getting Started in P1 mode.
