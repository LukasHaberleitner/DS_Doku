Replaying a Track

This task shows you how to replay a track using the Player pop-up toolbar. 

  1. Double-click the track created (i.e. Track.1) in the specification tree or click Track in the DMU Simulation toolbar.

    The Player pop-up toolbar is displayed.

    Player Pop-up Toolbar

  2. Click Parameters .

    The Player Parameters dialog box is displayed:

  3. Modify the Sampling Step and the Temporization values as desired.

  4. Use the VCR buttons or the slider to simulate your track.
