Toolbars

 

This section describes the various s available in the STL Rapid Prototyping workbench. Other menus are documented in the Infrastructure User's Guide.

The toolbars

  • STL Files
  • STL Edition
  • STL Mesh
  • STL Operations
  • STL Analysis

are located on the right in the default set-up.

The View Management tool bar is located at the bottom of the screen.
