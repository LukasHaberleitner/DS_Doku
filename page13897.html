Tessellating a Surface

The first task will show you how to enter the STL Rapid Prototyping  workbench
and create a basic surface tessellation.
The only pre-requisites for this task is to have a current session running.

  1. Select Start>NC Manufacturing> STL Rapid Prototyping.
    The STL Rapid Prototyping  workbench is displayed and ready to use.

  2. Open the GettingStartedSTL.CATPart from the samples directory.
    It is a hairdryer, made of surfaces, with a gap and a hole in it.

  3. Click Tessellation and select the hairdryer. Click Apply and OK.

    You can see clearly the hole and the gap in the tessellation.
