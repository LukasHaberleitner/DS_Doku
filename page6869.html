Entering the Workbench

 This task shows you how to enter the Electrical Connectivity Diagrams workbench.

  1. Choose the Electrical Connectivity Diagrams item from the Start -> Equipments & Systems -> Electrical Cabling Discipline menu.

    The New Drawing dialog box displays:
    It allows you to choose the standard and the format of your output drawing

  2. Click OK.

    The workbench displays, with a new Sheet.
