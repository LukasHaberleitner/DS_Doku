Checking Ply Rules in the Virtual Stacking

This task shows you how to check ply rules.
Either work on the part where you have defined cells or open the VirtualStackingGA01.CATPart document.

  1. Select a ply in the 2D viewer of the main dialog box and click Check ply rules in the Tools Palette.
    The Score dialog box is displayed. It is resizable.

  2. Go to the tab of interest to check the information.

  3. Click then an item to check its errors.

  4. Click then an item to check its parameters.

  5. Click to save your parameters under a .xml file.

  6. Once you have modified a parameter, click to update the data.
