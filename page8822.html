Conventions

Certain conventions are used in CATIA, ENOVIA and DELMIA documentation to help you recognize and understand important concepts and specifications. This page describes:

  • Graphics Conventions
  • Text Conventions
  • Mouse Usage
  • Navigation compass

Graphic Conventions

The three categories of graphic conventions used are as follows:

  • Graphic conventions used to structure the tasks
  • Graphic conventions used to indicate the configuration required
  • Graphic conventions used in the table of contents

Text Conventions

The following text conventions are used:

  • The titles of CATIA, ENOVIA and DELMIA documents appear in this manner throughout the text.
  • File > New identifies the commands to be used.
  • Enhancements are identified by a blue-colored background on the text.
