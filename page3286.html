Going to Hyperlinks 

This procedure describes how to go to a hyperlink in a product document.
 
To add a hyperlink to a product document, see add hyperlinks.
 
Product documents can be used as either products or resources within your process document.  Therefore, the contents of either type of product document can be edited.
 

  1. On the PPR tree, select the product that has a hyperlink.

  2. Click Go to Hyperlink . The system's internet browser opens to the hyperlink defined in the Manage Hyperlink dialog box.
