Connect/Disconnect Objects

This task shows you how to connect and disconnect an access object after you have placed it.      
1. To disconnect, click the Disconnect Access Part button and then select the part. A pop up box will ask if you want to disconnect. Click OK. The part is disconnected.
2. To connect, click the Connect Access Part button and then select the part you want to connect. Then follow the steps described from Step 2 of Placing a Door or Hatch on a Compartment.
