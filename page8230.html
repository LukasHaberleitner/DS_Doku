Global Posture Swap

Posture swaps may be done on any segment node and also on the manikin node in the specification tree.

To swap the entire posture, select the Body node in the specification tree. Right-click the mouse to activate the contextual menu and choose Posture > Swap.

The software swaps the posture by copying the posture of individual segments to the opposite side and vice versa. For instance, if the manikin is crossing its left leg before the swap, it will be crossing  its right knee after the swap.
