Removing the Mesh

This task shows you how to remove the mesh you generated on a surface geometry.
This functionality is available both in the Surface Mesher workshop and in the Advanced Surface Mesher workshop.
  • Open the sample06.CATAnalysis document from the samples directory.
  • Enter the Surface Mesher workshop or the Advanced Surface Mesher workshop.
     

  1. Launch the mesh operation.
    For more details, refer to Launching the Mesh Execution.

  2. Click Remove Mesh in the Execution toolbar.

    The surface mesh is removed.
