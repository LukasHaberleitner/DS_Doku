Optimal CATIA PLM Usability for Product Structure

Product Structure can be edited in CATIA V5. You need to have created in ENOVIA LCA and sent to CATIA V5 a Product Root Class (PRC) in order to begin to work. For more information about the PRC, please refer to the Engineering LifeCycle User's Guide, in the Getting Started: Creating a Product Class Root.

CATIA V5 thus becomes an editor of ENOVIA Product Structure. You can send to CATIA V5:

  • only the product structure, selecting in ENOVIA the Part Instances,
  • the product structure and its shape representations selecting in ENOVIA the attached document representations.

The purpose of this section is to prevent the user from building / editing data in CATIA V5 if it cannot be saved in ENOVIA LCA. Therefore, in interoperability mode, some CATIA V5 commands are dimmed or unavailable in the Product Structure workbench.

Only commands subject to restrictions are listed below. In some cases, rules are applied to restricted commands.
