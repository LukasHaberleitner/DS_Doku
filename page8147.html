Getting Started

The purpose of this tutorial is to teach you how to repair a surface to make a join with a Merging distance of 0.01mm.

You will:

  • Open the file,
  • Check the topology of the part,
  • Check the geometry of the part,
  • Repair the part,
  • Make a global join,
  • Check whether this join is closed or not and close it.
  Note that the values given in the dialog boxes may vary with the version used.
It should take you approximately 10 minutes to complete.
