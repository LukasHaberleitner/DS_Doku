Which license do I need to use the Supply Chain Engineering Exchange application?  

For the Supply Chain Engineering Exchange application to be available you will need the following license:

  • EWE for ENOVIAVPM 
  • WPE for ENOVIA V5 VPM

Can I access the Supply Chain Engineering Exchange application  from all workbenches ?  

The Supply Chain Engineering Exchange application can be accessed from the following workbenches:

  • Product Structure
  • Part Design
  • Generative Shape Design
  • Drafting
  • Generative Structural Analysis

To find out more, see Accessing the Supply Chain Engineering Exchange Application.
