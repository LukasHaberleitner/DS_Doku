Creating a Triangle

This task shows you how to create a triangular open surface made of three quadrangular faces.
A .CATPart document must be open.

  1. Click Triangle .
  A mesh is aligned along the model axis and the surface is created on the plane that is the most parallel to the screen.
 

  A Tools Palette appears to let you modify and edit the surface:
 
  Refer to Modifying and Deforming Surfaces for further information.
  The surface (identified as Subdivision Surface.x) is added to the specification tree.
