Zooming Out

 

This task explains how to zoom out by predetermined increments.

  • Select View > Modify > Zoom Out, or click Zoom Out in the View toolbar.

    You zoom out by one increment each time you click on the icon. To zoom out (as illustrated), click the icon several times in succession.
    Before After  

You can also select View > Zoom In Out and drag (left mouse button) to zoom out progressively, not by increments.
