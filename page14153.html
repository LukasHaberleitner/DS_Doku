ToolBars

These are the specific icon toolbars that belong to the Die Face Design workbench. 

The toolbars

  • Method Plan

  • Process Part Separation

  • Process Definition

  • Structure Definition

  • Preparation

  • Analysis

  • Part Extension

  • Addendum Design

  • Modify Fillet Radius

  • Compensation

  • Design Change

  • Selection Tools

are located on the right in the default set-up
while Selection Tools is on the top bar.
