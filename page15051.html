Creating Custom Reports

This section describes how you can create reports that display the attributes of objects you are working with. You can also customize the reports to suit your own needs.

You must enable the correct settings. Click Tools - Options - General - Parameters and Measure and click on the Language tab.  Under Language check Load extended language libraries. Either check All Packages, or uncheck this option and load the Knowledgeware packages you will be using.
