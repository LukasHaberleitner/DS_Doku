Entering the Electrical 3D Design
Assembly Workbench

The Electrical 3D Design Assembly workbench allows you to create electrical assemblies in CATProduct documents.
CATIA V5 is launched. A CATProduct document is displayed.

  1. Select Electrical 3D Design Assembly from the Start  >   Equipments & Systems  >  Electrical Harness Discipline menu.

    The Electrical 3D Design Assembly workbench is displayed and ready to use.
