Activating the Manikin Coloring Properties

 In the Profiles section of the manikin tree, the coloring node allows access to the coloring properties. These coloring properties will be activated, and as such, will allow you to see the direct effect of analysis on the segments of the manikin during simulation.

  1. Select the Coloring Properties node in the Profiles under the manikin tree.

  2. Select the Active to activate the coloring properties.

  3. Tune the appropriate coloring parameters in the panel, as required.
