Find Similar Parts

This task explains how to search for a part in Part Supply similar to a selected part in the Part Editor or the Product Editor. Ensure that you are connected to Part Supply.

  1. In Product Editor, right-click a part and select Components > Find similar in PartSupply, or in the Part editor, right-click a part and select Base object > Find similar in PartSupply. A warning message appears.

  2. Click Yes in the warning message.

    The side panel lists similar parts.

In Part Supply, you can open, insert, or replace a selected similar part.
