Moving Rows in the Stacking Management

This task shows you how to move rows directly from the Tools Palette in the Stacking Management.
Open a document with a stacking
 

  1. In the Tools Palette, click Move Row .

    Move rows inside a group (plies group, sequence, ply) or to a new group (except cut-pieces).

    • In the table, select the row to move.
    • Select the target row.
      • Move a ply inside or outside its sequence (a new sequence is created).
      • Move a sequence inside or outside its plies group (a new plies group is dreated).
      • Move a cut-piece inside its ply.

    The moved row is copied before the target row.

  2. Click Change Row Parent .

    • Available for plies or sequences.
    • Does not support filters
    • In the table, select the row to move.
    • Select the target row (sequence or plies group).

    The moved row is copied in the target.

  3. Click Swap Rows .
    • Available for any entities.
    • Does not support filters
    • Rows are swapped only inside the same group.
