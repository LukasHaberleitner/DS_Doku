Moving the End Manipulator of a Leader

This task shows you how to move the end manipulator of a leader.
  • You can move a end manipulator of a leader on geometrical elements associated with the annotation only.
  • To move the manipulator freely across the annotation plane, select the Freely move leader extremity on geometry check box at Tools > Options > Mechanical Design > Functional Tolerancing & Annotation > Manipulators. This option is applicable to annotations with only one leader that has only one extremity and pointing geometry.
  • Yellow geometrical elements appear to plot the route on them where the end manipulator is moveable.
    These geometrical elements represent the intersection between the geometry and the annotation's annotation plane, and consist of point/line/curve. Of course, if the intersection consists of a point, you cannot move the end manipulator.
Open the Annotations_Part_04.CATPart document:
  • Improve the highlight of the related geometry, see Highlighting of the Related Geometry for 3D Annotation.

  1. Click the annotation text.

  2. Select the end manipulator to be moved.

  3. Drag the end manipulator along the yellow line.

  4. Release the end manipulator.

    You obtain this result.

    • When you add an extremity link, the extremity points cannot be moved but when you move the annotations, leaders are adjusted accordingly.
    • When the breakpoint is added, according to the position of the breakpoint, the leader extremity is adjusted.
