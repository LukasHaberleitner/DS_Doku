Transferring Existing Annotations 

This task shows you how to transfer an existing textual annotation from one view to another.
The operating mode described here applies to datum elements, datum targets and geometrical tolerances too.
Open the Annotations_Part_04.CATPart document:
  • Improve the highlight of the related geometry, see Highlighting of the Related Geometry for 3D Annotation.

  1. Select the text (or its leader line).

  2. Right-click to select the Transfer to View/Annotation Plane contextual menu.

  1. Select Front View.2 in the tree or in the geometry. You can perform the operation on both active and non-active views.

    The textual annotation is transferred to the new view.
