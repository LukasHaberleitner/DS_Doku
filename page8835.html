Creating a Pyramid

This task shows you how to create a pyramid made of three triangular equilateral surfaces, each of them made of three quadrangular faces. A weight is applied on all edges and vertices, except internal edges and vertices.
A .CATPart document must be open.

  1. Click Pyramid .
  A mesh is aligned along the model axis:
 

  A Tools Palette appears to let you modify and edit the surface:
 
  Refer to Modifying and Deforming Surfaces for further information.
  The surface (identified as Subdivision Surface.x) is added to the specification tree.
