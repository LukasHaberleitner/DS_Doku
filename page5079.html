Graphic Replacement for Slots

When you generate graphic replacement for stiffeners and plates, the slot symbols are drawn depending on the type of slot.

 In the sample XML file, the generative view style parameters for slots are located under the following sections of Stiffener and Plate StructureObjects:

AnyView section of Plate

SideView section of Stiffener
  SlotSymbol
Draw
Draws slot symbols. Yes or No.
ReferenceScale
Scale factor to apply to the symbol as drawn. Default is 100.
ChapterName
The name of the chapter containing slot symbols within the Drawing Symbol Structure Catalog. (Refer to PRM file.)
SymbolBySlotType
Tee_Slot_thru_Plate (This is the name of the slot in the catalog)
The symbol used for a Tee slot through plate.
Tee_Slot_thru_Shape
The symbol used for a Tee slot through shape.
Welded_Slot_for_Tee
The symbol used for a welded slot for Tee.
CC-10
The symbol used for a CC-10 slot.
CC-10-2Tee_thru_Shape
The symbol used for a CC-10-2Tee slot through shape.
Smart_Param_CC-10
The symbol used for a Smart Param CC-10 slot.
