Rotate Resource Using the Definition Panel

  This task shows you how to rotate a catalog resource by entering into the definition panel the number of degrees that you want it to rotate on its vertical axis..
1. Click on the resource..
2. Click Edit-Definition in the menu bar. This displays the Definition panel. (You can also enter Cntrl-Enter to display the panel.)
3. Enter the degrees in the Rotation space.
4. Click Apply and the resource will rotate on its vertical axis.
