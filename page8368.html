Removing an User-defined Population

Follow these steps to remove a user-defined population from Human Measurements Editor:
 
  1. Choose Tools > Options from the main menu. Go to the Ergonomics Design & Analysis section, then select Human Measurements Editor.
     
  2. In the User-defined populations field, select the items to remove, then select the Remove button.

     
     
  3. OK to close the Options dialog box; the populations are removed.
