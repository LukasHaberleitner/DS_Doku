Navigating in Examine Mode

 Navigating in Examine Mode is the default navigation mode. You can examine your document as you would from the outside by moving around the document's perimeter, or as you would from within, modifying your orientation (turn head) or moving closer to different objects (zoom in, zoom out).
This task shows you how to rotate, zoom and move your document.

  1. To rotate, press and hold the Middle Mouse button, then press the Left Mouse button and drag (still holding both buttons down).

  1. To zoom, press and hold the Middle Mouse button, then click the Left Mouse button and drag (still holding the Middle Mouse button down). 

Dragging towards 12 o'clock zooms in on your  document; dragging towards 6 o'clock zooms out from your document.
