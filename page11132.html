CATTPSAllAnnotations  

Enables you to retrieve all the annotations contained in the document from an Expert Rule.

Syntax

'Functional Tolerancing & Annotations'.Annotation

Example

Retrieves all the annotations in the document.

'Functional Tolerancing & Annotations'.Annotation

CATTPSCapture

Enables you to retrieve all the annotation captures contained in the document from an Expert Rule.

Syntax

'Functional Tolerancing & Annotations'.Capture

Example

Retrieves all the annotation captures in the document.

'Functional Tolerancing & Annotations'.Capture
