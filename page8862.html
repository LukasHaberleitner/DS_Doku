Smoothing a Curve

This task shows you how to smooth a curve in order to limit its defects.

Open the ModifyCurve3.CATPart document.

  1. Click Modification .

  2. Click the curve.

  3. Click Smoothing .

  4. Click the geometry area to apply the smoothing.

  5. Click as many times as you wish to reach the desired level of smoothness.
