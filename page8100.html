Getting Started

The following tutorial aims at giving you a feel of what you can do with FreeStyle Shaper & Optimizer. It provides step-by-step scenarios showing you how to use key capabilities.

The main tasks proposed in this section are:

Entering the Workbench
Creating a First Surface
Editing the Surface
Creating a Second Surface
Creating a Blend Between Surfaces
Applying a Global Deformation
All together, this scenario should take about 15 minutes to complete.
