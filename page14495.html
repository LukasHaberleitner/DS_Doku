Pushing Route Segments

This task shows how to push route segments.
You can only push a route segment in a direction that is orthogonal to the route segment.
1. Select a route. The segments that can be pushed display push segment manipulators.

In the above example, the three segments that can be pushed highlight and display the push segment manipulators.
2. Drag the push segment manipulator on a route segment in the appropriate direction.

In the above example, pushing the horizontal segment reshaped the two vertical segments.
