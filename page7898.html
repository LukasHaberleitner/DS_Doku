Setting Global Meshing Parameters

This task shows you how to visualize or modify the global meshing parameters at any time in the Advanced Surface Mesher workshop (before and after simplifying the geometry, before and after meshing).

Open the sample06.CATAnalysis document from the samples directory.

  • Enter the Advanced Surface Mesher workshop.
    For more details, refer to Entering the Advanced Surface Mesher Workshop.

  1. Click Global Meshing Parameters in the Global Specifications toolbar.

    The Global Parameters dialog box appears.

    For more details about the description of the Global Parameters dialog box, refer to Entering the Advanced Surface Mesher Workshop.

  2. Change the desired parameters.

  3. Click OK.
