Managing Drawings

SMARTEAM provides powerful tools to assist you in creating, saving and managing your drawings.

When you save the drawing into the SMARTEAM database, a general link is automatically created between the drawing and the part/product. This enables you to manage the drawing together with the part/product as you create revisions.
In this manner, the status of the drawing remains parallel with the status of the document on which it was based and SMARTEAM protects the integrity of the drawing.
