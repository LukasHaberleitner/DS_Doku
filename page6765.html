Creating Protective Coverings

Defining Protection Products:
  • This command exists in the Electrical Assembly Design workbench.
  • It allows you to create a more flexible protective covering.
  • You can define four types of protection products.
  • There is no geometry created.
  • Customization (attributes, parameters) is now supported during instantiation.
   
Defining Protection Parts:
  • This command exists in the Electrical Part Design workbench.
  • You can define two types of protection parts.
  • The associated geometry is created by default.
  • Customization (geometry, attributes, parameters) is supported during instantiation.
