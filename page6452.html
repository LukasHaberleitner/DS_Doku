Entering the Electrical 3D Design
Part Workbench

The Electrical 3D Design Part workbench is used to define electrical connectors or to convert standard parts into electrical devices.
CATIA V5 is launched. A CATPart document is displayed.

  1. Select Electrical 3D Design Part from the Start  >   Equipments & Systems   >  Electrical Harness Discipline menu.

    The Electrical 3D Design Part workbench is displayed with a CATPart document.

  2. Click Exit to exit the part workbench and return to the assembly workbench.
