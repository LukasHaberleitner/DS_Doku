Resizing Infinite Elements

 

You can resize the graphical representation of features with infinite geometry.
If the Resize Infinite Elements option in the Tools toolbar is selected, manipulators are available on selected features with infinite geometry allowing to resize infinite elements.

Open the CATPart document containing infinite element, e.g. infinite planes.

  1. In the Tools toolbar, click Resize Infinite Elements .

  2. Select an infinite plane, e.g. xy plane.
    Manipulators are displayed on the plane.

  3. Resize the infinite plane by drawing one of the manipulators.

  4. Move the cursor onto a plane edge until its shape changes to .
    You can now move the plane.

  5. Click the Reset resizing to restore the original shape.
