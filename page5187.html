Accessing Tessellation Error Messages

 

Messages generated by tessellation errors are accessible via an information button at the bottom-right corner of the Web Viewer.

At the beginning of your session, the information button is deactivated. If a tessellation error occurs, the button is activated and will remain so until the end of your session.

  1. If a tessellation error occurs and the information    button at the bottom-right corner of the Web Viewer is activated, click the button in order to see the error message.
    The error message appears in the Viewer Space.


     

  2. To remove the message, click anywhere within the message box.

The stack of messages has a maximum size of 6. Each click on the button will step you backwards in the stack of messages.
