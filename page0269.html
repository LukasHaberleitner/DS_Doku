Getting the Most Out of the Documentation

To get the most out of the Abaqus for CATIA V5 documentation, we suggest that you start by reading and performing the step-by-step tasks in the tutorial chapter, Getting Started. The chapters that follow present each of the main capabilities in Abaqus for CATIA V5 in the form of basic tasks. It may also be helpful to take a look at Nonlinear Structural Analysis Workbench Description and Thermal Analysis Workbench Description, which outline the Abaqus for CATIA V5 toolbars.
