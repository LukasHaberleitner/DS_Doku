DMU Simulation Toolbar

The DMU Simulation toolbar has one sub-toolbar: Other Actions. The Manipulation pop-up toolbar appears when you select the Shuttle , Explode , Generate Track , or Track .  Depending on the command that triggers the Manipulation toolbar's appearance, you see different Manipulation pop-up toolbars: one during shuttle definition (also Explode ) and another during trajectory definition. The Recorder pop-up toolbar appears when you select Track .  The hyperlinks below link to the procedures that describe how to use the commands in the DMU Simulation toolbar and its associated sub-toolbar and pop-up toolbars.
