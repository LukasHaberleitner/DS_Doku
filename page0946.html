The Postural Score  

This page deals with the Postural Score options.

 

Postural Score
   
 
  • Choose one or select more than one using the Ctrl key.
  • Move them back and forth from the Available field to the Favorites field with the and buttons.
  • Use the and buttons to move entire lists back and forth.
  • Use the buttons to move favorite segments up or down in the sorting order.
  • Use the pull-down menu to select the bar colors for the Score.
