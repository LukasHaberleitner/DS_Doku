Marker2D Object

See Also UseCases Properties Methods

 
 

Marker2D objects are object drawn on an annotated view. Different types of Marker2D objects are available :

line: a simple line between two points
arrow: an arrow starting from one point and finishing on another
rectangle: a rectangle between two points
circle: a circle with one point as center and passing thru another one
free-hand: a curve passing thru points
text: a string of characters whith typographic attributes, anchored on a point
picture: a picture drawn between two points

Depending on its type, attributes of a marker can be modified using different methods and properties.

Marker2D objects are managed thru a Marker2Ds collection.
