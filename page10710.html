Text Detail

This task shows you how V4 Text Detail is copied As Result into CATIA V5.
Open the document DITTOSYMBOL.model in CATIA V5.

And press the *DRAFT tab to visualize the Drawing view of the Text Detail:

  1. Copy / Paste the model into a CATDrawing document.

V4 Text Details are converted into V5 Text Details in CATIA V5.

You can edit and modify the Text Detail in the V5 CATDrawing; it is represented by the broken lines around the Text Detail: . The broken lines turn green when you double click this text to modify it and the Text Editor dialog box appears:

You cannot modify the Ditto called "texte" in this *DRAFT View:

If you double-click "texte" in the *DRAFT tab, the DETPBU becomes active and you can modify the text.

  1. Apply a Check As Result on the .model only when you notice that an entity cannot be pasted.
