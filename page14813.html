Tooltips in the Specifications Tree

When you move your mouse pointer over a functional object in the specifications tree, you will see that tooltips display statistics. The following table lists what those statistics refer to:  

SuperPlate: Support offset / Material Grade / Thickness

Plate: Support / Material Grade / Thickness

SuperStiffener: Support offset / Material Grade / Section

Stiffener: Support offset / Material Grade / Section

SuperStiffenerOnFreeEdge/Beam: Material Grade / Section

StiffenerOnFreeEdge/Beam: Material Grade /Section
