Toolbars

 

This section describes the various icons available in the Shape Sculptor workbench.

The toolbars

  • Input/Output
  • Component Selection
  • Creation
  • Editing
  • Modeling
  • Analysis
  • Wireframe
  • Compass
are located on the right in the default set-up.

The View Management tool bar is located at the bottom of the screen.
You can also  access the Terrain Modeling toolbar.
