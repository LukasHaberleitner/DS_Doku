What is Loaded from the Manufacturing Hub into DPM

When a process is loaded into DPM through the Manufacturing Hub, the following entities are imported into Process and Resource Definition:

  • The selected process and its parents (for context information)
  • All sub-processes of the selected process
  • All products (and their sub-structures) assigned to the selected process and its sub-processes
  • The parents of the associated products (for context information)
  • The fasteners and locators linked to the loaded products (see the Loading Locators and Fasteners section for more details)
  • All resources (and their sub-structures) assigned to the selected process and its sub-processes
  • The parents of the associated resources (for context information)
  • The Process Graph for the selected process (viewable in Process and Resource Definition via the PERT chart)

Note: Products, fasteners, locators, and resources are loaded only if their association to the loaded processes is of a supported type. See the Supported Process Relationship Types section to access to the supported relations list.
