Managing Annotated Views

 

At any time, you can access the different annotated views you've associated to your 2D document. 
Open the CompleteDrawing.cgm from the samples folder.
You've already created one or more annotated views.

 

  1. In the DMU 2D Tools toolbar, click the Manage Annotated Views icon .

The Annotated Views dialog box appears.

  1. Double-click the view you wish to work with.

The chosen view becomes the active view. All of the annotations that you previously created in that view will be visible.
