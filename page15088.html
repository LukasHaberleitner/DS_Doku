Customizing Reconciliation Settings

Before you start your first working session, you can customize the way you work to suit your habits.
This type of customization is stored in permanent setting files: these settings will not be lost if you end your session

  1. Select the Tools  >  Options command. The Options dialog box displays.

  2. Choose the Infrastructure  >  Product Structure category in the left-hand box.
    The options for Product Structure settings appear, organized in tab pages.

  3. Click the Reconciliation  tab.

    The Reconciliation tab enables you define the Reconciliation settings.

  4. Click the Cache Management  tab.


     

     The Cache Management tab enables you to customize cache settings.

  5. Set options in these tabs according to your needs.

  6. Click OK in the Options dialog box to confirm the chosen options.
