Navigating in Examine Mode

 Navigating in Examine Mode is the default mode. You can examine your document as you would from the outside by moving around the document's perimeter, or as you would from within, turning your head to view or moving closer (zoom in, zoom out) to different objects.

For more information, see Activating Viewing Tools Using the Mouse.
You can also access the Examine Mode by clicking Examine Mode in the View toolbar, available when using the beginners walk and fly modes.
