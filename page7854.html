Solid Meshing

This section deals with the 3D meshing methods.

The following functionalities are only available with the FEM Solid (FMD) product.

Creating Octree 3D Mesh Parts: Assign linear or parabolic tetrahedrons elements to a solid.

Tetrahedron filler: Mesh a solid part by generating tetrahedron meshes from surface meshes.

Sweep 3D: Mesh a solid part by generating hexahedron and wedge elements from surface meshes.
