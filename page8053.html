Splitting the Pad

This task will show you how to split the cylinder by the surface.

  1. Now, choose Part Design from the Start > Mechanical Design menu. The Part Design workbench is displayed again.

  1. Select the cylinder and click the Split icon:

  1. Select the created surface as the splitting plane.
 

An arrow appears indicating the portion of body that will be kept, make sure that it points in the right direction.
 
 

  1. Click OK in the Split Definition dialog box.
 

The cylinder has been split by the surface.
