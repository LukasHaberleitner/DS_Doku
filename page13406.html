Redefining an Automatic Corner Relief

This task explains how to redefine automatic corner relieves on a sheet metal part.

Open the CornerRelief01.CAPTPart model from the samples directory.

  1. Double-click the bend on which the corner relief you want to redefine is located.

It may be easier to double-click it from the specification tree.

The Bend Definition dialog box is displayed.

  1. Click the More button and select the Bend Corner Relief tab.

This tab is similar to the Corner Relief tab of Sheet Metal Parameters dialog box.

  1. Choose a new corner relief type, and click OK to validate. 

This definition will apply to the current corner relief, and will prevail over any other corner relief definition you may enter through the Sheet Metal Parameter dialog box.
