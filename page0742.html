The DMU 2D Workshop settings page concerns the following settings categories:

  • Background

 

Background

 

Graduated color background

  
  If you wish the background color to be graduated, click the Graduated color background selection box.

By default, this option is unchecked.

  
     
 

Background color

  
  Click on the combo-box selection button and choose the desired standard color from the proposed list.

By default, this option is valuated to White.
