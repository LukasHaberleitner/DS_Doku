Creating an Item Reservation on a Defined Plane

  This task shows you how to place an item reservation on a defined plane.
When you create an item reservation it is normally placed on the base plane. This function allows you to place it at any plane or location in your document.
1. Click the Item Reservation button . The Item Reservation Creation dialog box shows.
2. Click the On Surface button . 
3. Move your mouse pointer and click at the location where you want to place the item reservation - a white square shows as you move the pointer. Note: you cannot place an item reservation in free space or on an area using the On Surface option.
