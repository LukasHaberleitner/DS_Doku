Manipulating a Curve

This task shows you how to deform a curve by pushing and pulling it.

Open the ModifyCurve2.CATPart document.

  1. Click Modification .

  2. Click the curve.

  3. Click Manipulation .

  4. Move the mouse to place the running point at the desired position.

  5. Press the left-mouse button and move the mouse to deform the curve.

  6. Release the left-mouse button when you are satisfied with the deformation.
