Performing Shape Operations

When working in the Imagine and Shape workbench, the Shape Operations toolbar is available to let you access a set of functions belonging to the Generative Shape Design workbench.
Here are the available functions:
  • Boundary
  • Extract
  • Multiple Extract (refer to the Generative Shape Design User's Guide)
  • Trim
  • Split
  • Shape Fillet (refer to the Generative Shape Design User's Guide)
  • Edge Fillet (refer to the Generative Shape Design User's Guide)
  • Variable Fillet (refer to the Generative Shape Design User's Guide)
  • Join
  • Offset (refer to the Generative Shape Design User's Guide)
  • Intersection
  • Extrude
  • Revolve
  • Blend
