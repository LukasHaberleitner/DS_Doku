Scaling Bundle Segments

This task shows you how to enter a bundle segment fake length. Working with fake lengths enables you to fit the whole harness in the board.
The document contains the flattened harness obtained in the previous task.

  1. Click Scale .

    You are prompted to select a bundle segment in the geometry area.
    The Scale dialog box pops up.
    The bundle segment true length is indicated.

  2. Enter the fake length you want.

  3. Click OK to validate.

    The support relative positions are maintained when working with fake lengths.
    If you want to work with true lengths again, click Restore True Length.
