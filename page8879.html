Snapping Vertices from a Sub-division Surface to Another One

This task shows you how to snap the vertices from a sub-division surface onto the vertices of another sub-division surface.
A .CATPart document must be open.

  1. Multi-select the sub-division surfaces.

  2. To change into edition mode, press Space.

  3. From the Tools Palette, select any one of the following:

    • Multi Snap
    • Fitting Snap

  4. Click the source vertex and then click the target vertex.

  5. Perform the same operation for each vertex.



    The vertices are snapped to the target vertices but the two sub-division surfaces are not joined.

    • You can also snap all the vertices at the same time using the loop propagation option from the tools palette.
    • Select the direction of the loop arrow on the target vertex.
      This direction should be same as that of the source vertex.
