Sliding In-Line Components

This task explains how to slide an in-line component along a route and around route corners. The internal flow of the in-line component must be horizontal or vertical.
When you slide an in-line component along a route, you retain the shape (length and direction) of the line segments.

  Select an in-line component. A slide manipulator () displays beside the component.

Drag the slide manipulator to slide the component along a route.

As you slide the component, you retain the shape of the line segments.

You cannot slide a component past another branch, component, or route-to-route connection. You also cannot slide a component if it has more than two connections.

You can slide a component around route corners.

You cannot slide a component around a route corner when there is not enough room on the route segment to accommodate the component.
