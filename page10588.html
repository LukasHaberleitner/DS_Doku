Alphanumerical Reorder of DETAILS

In CATIA Model Editor, DETAILS are not alphanumerically ordered. This new functionality consists in restoring V4 behavior, that is to say the alphanumerical reorder of DETAIL tabs. This reordered DETAIL tabs will enable you to quickly find a given DETAIL in a large list.

The reorder operation is automatically activated when opening a model. This reorder is used in two workbenches:

  • CATIA V5 Model Editor
  • DMU Navigator (only 2D workspaces are shown).
