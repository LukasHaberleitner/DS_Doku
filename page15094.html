Delta mode

 

 

A VPDM system where to save the data: ENOVIA VPM V4, or ENOVIA V5 VPM if necessary.

 

The objective of the Delta mode is to enable a supplier or an OEM to exchange only the documents that have been modified since the previous exchange.
