MULTICAx JT Plug-in in a Nutshell

The MULTICAx JT Plug-in allows users to import JT data using Insert > Existing Component in Product Structure.

Before Reading this Guide

Prior to reading the MULTICAx JT Plug-in User's Guide, you are recommended to have a look at the Infrastructure User's Guide for  information on the generic capabilities common to all products.

  

Getting the Most Out of this Guide

To make the most out of this book, we suggest that a beginning user reads the Requirements chapter first of all and the other Users Tasks to find his way around the  MULTICAx JT Plug-in.

Accessing Sample Documents

To perform the scenarios, sample documents are provided all along this documentation. For more information about this, refer to Accessing Sample Documents in the Infrastructure User's Guide.
