Tool Bars

 

This section describes the various icons available in the Healing Assistant workbench.

The toolbars

  • Check Topology

  • Check Geometry

  • Repair Topology

  • Repair Geometry

  • Sketch

  • Wireframe

  • Surfaces

  • Operations

  • Shape Analysis

are located on the right in the default set-up

The Generic Tools icons are located in the horizontal top toolbar.
