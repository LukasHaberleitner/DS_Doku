Viewing Cable Layout

This task shows you how to view the layout of cables on a hanger.
1. If you want you can hide the loft for better viewing. To Hide, select the loft in the specifications tree, click the right mouse button, and select Hide/Show in the drop down menu.
2. Click the Cable Visualization button . The Cable Visualization dialog box displays.
3. Select the hanger whose layout you want to see. The cables display on the hanger and their IDs display in the Cable Visualization box.
