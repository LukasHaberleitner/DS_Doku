Customizing

Before you start your first working session, you can customize the way you work to suit your needs.
This type of customization deals with permanent setting customization: these settings will not be lost if you end your session.

  1. Select the Tools > Options menu item.

    The Options dialog box appears.

  1. Click the Digital Mockup category in the left-hand box.

  2. Click the DMU Fastening Review workbench.
    The General and the Display tabs are displayed.
 
  • The General tab lets you define the application and extract parameters, as well as the export and report directories.
  • The Display tab lets you define the display options.
 

  1. Set options in these tabs according to your needs.

  2. Click OK when done.
