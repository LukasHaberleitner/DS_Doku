Modifying Created Surfaces

The task shows you how to modify the two surfaces you have just created.

  1. Click the Control Points icon in the Shape Modification toolbar.
 

  1. Select the original surface.
    The mesh of Control Points appears.
 

  1. Now modify the surface.
    Because of the associativity, the flange surface changes accordingly.
 

 
 

  1. Close the Control Points dialog box.
