Creating a No-Drop-Off Area

This task shows you how to create a No Drop-Off Area.
Open NoDropoffGA01.CATPart from the samples directory.

You are going to create No Drop-Off Area from that contour and from the plies group.

  1. Click No Drop Off Area in the Plies toolbar.
    The No Drop Off Area dialog box is displayed.

  2. Select the Contour.1 as the Contour that defines the area,
    and the Plies Group.1 as the plies group it belongs to.

  3. Click OK to validate and exit the dialog box.
    A No Drop Off Area.1 is created:
