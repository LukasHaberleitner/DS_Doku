Customizing the Reconciliation Transaction

This section gives you information about the Supply Chain Engineering Exchange Application customization.

Can I inhibit some reconciliation rules?  

Yes. To do so, access the Reconciliation tools options (Tools > Options > Infrastructure > Product Structure > Reconciliation), and uncheck the reconciliation rule that you want to inhibit.

Note that you must re-launch the CATIA session in order that the modified settings be taken into account.
