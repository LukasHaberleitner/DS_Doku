Launching the Mesh Operation

This task will show you how to generate the mesh, now that you defined the global parameters and the specifications.

  1. Click Mesh The Part in the Execution toolbar.

    The mesh is generated on the part and a little summary is provided in the Mesh The Part dialog box.

     

    The visualization is switched to quality mode so that you can visualize the generated mesh and the quality of each element.

  2. Click OK.
