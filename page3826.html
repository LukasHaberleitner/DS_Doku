Transformation Behavior in an Electrical Assembly

This task explains the transformation behavior in an electrical assembly.

Open a product structure containing a flexible electrical assembly (electrical geometrical bundle).
 

Transformation in a Flexible Assembly

  1. Right-click the electrical geometrical bundle in the specification tree and open it in a new window.

  2. Place the compass on a connector in a flexible electrical geometry.

  3. Move the compass to move the connector.

       

  4. Click Update All to update.
 
  • The transformation on the connector instance in the Root context is penetrated into the Geometrical Bundle Reference context.
  • It is critical to retain the exact same length of dependent branches in any context (Root, Sub-product, Geometrical Bundle Reference etc).
  • This behavior is available only for an electrical flexible assembly of type Geometrical Bundle.
