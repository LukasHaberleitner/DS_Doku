Defining a Fixed Part

This task shows you how to define a Fixed part.

  1. Click Fixed Part in the DMU Kinematics toolbar or select Insert > Fixed Part... from the menu bar.The New Fixed Part dialog box appears.

  2. Select a fixed part either in the geometry area or in the specification tree.

     

    The fixed part is identified in the specification tree.

 

Your mechanism can be simulated. A warning message is displayed.

At any time you can click Undo to reverse your last action (here your selection).
