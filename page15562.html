Saving a DMU Review in Technological Package Context

 

All DMU Reviews contained in Technological Packages will be automatically saved when you do a Save in ENOVIA V5 VPM.
 

  1. In ENOVIA V5 VPM, click the Save icon.
    All DMU Reviews and their associated applicative data are automatically saved in the Technological Packages. They will all be available for access at a later time.
     

Note: Hyperlinks to objects are only exposed if the hyperlink is to a product instance. Hyperlinks to other objects will not be exposed.
