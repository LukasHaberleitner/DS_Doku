Erasing a Part of the Curve

This task shows you how to erase a part of the curve by painting the area to remove.

Open the ModifyCurve3.CATPart document.

  1. Click Modification .

  2. Click the curve.

  3. Click Erasing .

  4. Paint the area of the curve to be removed.
 

Removing the extremity of the curve
 

If the area includes the extremity of the curve, it is simply removed.
 

 

Removing an internal part of the curve
  If the area is internal to the curve, all details are erased to obtain a smoothed internal curve.
