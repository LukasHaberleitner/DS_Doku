Pyramids, Volumes and Project Primitives

This task shows you how pyramids but also volumes and project primitives are migrated into CATIA V5 using the option CATIA_SPEC.
Open the document PYRAMID+VOLUME+PROJECT.model in CATIA V5.

  1. Apply a Check AS SPEC on the solid.

  1. Copy and Paste Special *SOL40 into another CATPart document using the CATIA_SPEC option and update your document to compute the geometry.

You have access to the model's geometry only.
