Creating Bends

In this task, you will learn how to create bends between walls.

  1. Click Bend . The Bend Definition dialog box is displayed.

  2. Select Wall.1 as Support 1 and Wall on Edge.1/Face.1 as Support 2 in the specification tree or in the geometry area.

  3. Click OK. The bend is created.

  4. Repeat steps 1 to 3, this time selecting Wall.1 as Support 1 and Wall on Edge.2/Face.2 as Support 2. The second bend is created.

  5. Repeat steps 1 to 3, this time selecting Wall.1 as Support 1 and Wall on Edge.3/Face.3 as Support 2. The third bend is created.

    The bend features are displayed in the specification tree: Cylindrical Bend.1, Cylindrical Bend.2 and Cylindrical Bend.3.
