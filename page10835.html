Defining a Fixed Part

This task will show you how to define a fixed part.

Open the rods+4joints+cmd.CATProduct document.

  1. Click Fixed Part in the Simulation toolbar or select Insert > Fixed Part... from the menu bar. The New Fixed Part dialog box is displayed.

  2. Select the Fixed Part either in the geometry area or in the specification tree.

  3. The fixed Part is automatically defined and identified in the specification tree.

Keep in mind you can use Undo to modify your selection.
