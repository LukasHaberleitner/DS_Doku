Aligning Icons

This task explains how to align icons. The icons are aligned to the nearest grid location on the display screen.
Open the MaterialReplace.CATMaterial document.

 

  1. Drag some of the icons to place them in a haphazard manner on the display screen.

  2. Click Align Icons .

    The icons are rearranged, and placed at the nearest grid location. However, the number of icons per row is not customizable.
