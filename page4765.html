Displaying Information

This task shows you how to get information on a cloud of points.
Open Info1.CATPart from the samples directory.

  1. Click Information and select a cloud (cloud of points or mesh).

  2. An information box is displayed, with the statistics about the selected cloud:
    • Number of cells
    • Number of points, of visible points, of activated  and of filtered points
    • Number of triangles (if any) and of boundaries (if any)
    • Coordinates of the main extremities of the bounding box and its dimensions.

  3. If you select another cloud, the information box is updated with the statistics of that cloud.

  4. Click Close when you are finished to exit the action.
