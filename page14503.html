Querying a Zone

This task shows you how to query a zone for its members, or query a member to see which zone it belongs to.

1.

 With your document open click the Select/Query Zone button . The Selecting Zones dialog box will display.

2. Check Local under Scope if you want to see all zones in the document. Check All if you want to see all zones available to you.
3. Use the Filter and Sort functions as needed.
4. To see which zone the boundary is part of, check Zone ID under Selection Type and click on the boundary. The zone will highlight.  When you click on the zone all boundaries that are part of it will highlight.
5. To see which objects are part of the zone check Zone ID Members and then click on the zone.
