CatSectionPlaneNormal (Enumeration)

enum CatSectionPlaneNormal {
  catSectionNormal_X,
  catSectionNormal_Y,
  catSectionNormal_Z
}
The different default normals for the section plane.
It is used by the object to specify the plane's normal in settings.
Values:
catSectionNormal_X
The normal of the section plane is along the X axis.
catSectionNormal_Y
The normal of the section plane is along the Y axis.
catSectionNormal_Z
The normal of the section plane is along the Z axis.
