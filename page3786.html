Creating Centered Rectangles

This task shows you how to create:
  • a centered rectangle
  • a constrained centered rectangle

Creating a Centered Rectangle

  1. Ensure that the Geometrical Constraints and the Dimensional Constraints options are deactivated then click Centered Rectangle .

  2. Click a point in the geometry area or select an existing one.

  1. Drag the cursor to create the centered rectangle, then click when you get the size you want.
 
 

Creating a Constrained Centered Rectangle

To set constraints while creating a centered rectangle, first activate Geometrical Constraints and Dimensional Constraints (activated by default), then follow the same steps as explained above.

Equidistant constraints are applied automatically on the opposed lines accordingly to the center point.
