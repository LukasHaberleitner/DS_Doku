Receiving Documents

 

You load any documents you've received via the Document Sharing dialog box.

  1. Click the Document sharing icon or in the Community dialog box, right-click
    The Document sharing dialog box appears.
     

  2. Click the Import tab.
    A document dispatched by another user will appear with status Proposed.



     

  3. To accept a proposed document, click the Accept button.
    The status of the document will change to Transferred.

  4. To open the transferred document, click the Open button.
    The jpg document corresponding to the snapshot will be opened in your viewer.

  5. To refuse a proposed document, in the Document Sharing dialog box, click the Refuse button.
