Surface Meshing

This section deals with the 2D meshing methods.

Creating OCTREE 2D Mesh Parts: Assign linear or parabolic triangle elements to a surface.

Surface Mesher: Mesh a surface part and let you enter the Surface Mesher workshop.

Advanced Surface Mesher: Mesh a surface part and let you enter the Advanced Surface Mesher workshop.

Rule Based Surface Mesher: Mesh a surface part using parameters that are defined in a mesh rule file. Only available with the Rule Based Meshing (RBM) product.
