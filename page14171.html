Migrating V4 Models to V5

This section discusses migrating your V4 Tubing Design models to V5. The four steps needed to do this are:

  • Set up directories in which you can store your migrated data.
  • Migrate tubing parts to V5.
  • Edit the tubing mapping table.
  • Migrate the V4 model into V5.

To learn how to customize line thickness, see documentation for Interactive Drafting in the section Advanced Tasks/Manage Standards. Once defined, line thickness is applied to elements as a property.
