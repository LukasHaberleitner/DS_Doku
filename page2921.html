Synchronizing Chapters  

 

This task explains how to synchronize chapters.

The ALL_FASTENERS.catalog should be opened.

  1. In the Catalog tree, select the chapters to be synchronized.

  2. In the Data toolbar, click the Synchronize Chapters icon .
    The Synchronize Chapters dialog box appears.

  3. Click the Synchronize button.
    The selected chapters are synchronized.
    The results of the synchronization appear in the Synchronization report.
To extend the synchronization process to sub-catalogs pointed to in external catalogs, click the Propagate to sub-catalogs check box.
