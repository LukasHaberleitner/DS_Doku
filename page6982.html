Modify Location of Equipment

This task shows you how to move equipment.
1. Make the equipment's parent the active element.
2. Click on the equipment.
The item is highlighted.
3. Place your cursor at the desired location on the equipment.

As you move the cursor over the equipment, visual "cues" are displayed to show how you can move it.

  • A plane is displayed when the cursor is over a line to show that you can move the equipment  along that line.
  • A cross hair is displayed when the cursor is on a corner. From a corner, you can move the equipment in any direction.
4. Drag the item reservation to a new location.
5. Select the Update icon to see the modification.
