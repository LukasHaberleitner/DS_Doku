About Inspect Processes Created Before Release 16

In release 16, the structure of processes was altered.  Inspection station activities are the highest level of each process; activities performed by a DME are the children of each DME. 
  Users simply open any processes created prior to release 16 within release 16.  Inspect automatically reconfigures the process so that all of the activities become the children of either a single-arm or dual-arm CMM activities.
