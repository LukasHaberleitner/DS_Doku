Saving and Printing Image Captures

 

You can save and print image captures of your 2D documents. This enables you to save and eventually communicate your annotated views and your comparisons between 2D documents to other users.

  1. In the menu bar, select Tools  >  Image  >  Capture.

     

The Capture toolbar appears.

 

  1. To save in pixel format, click Pixel Mode  and then click Save As  .

  1. To save in vectorial format, click Vectorial Mode   and then click  Save As  .

  1. To print your 2D document, click Print  .

For detailed information on using the Capture toolbar, including the save and print functionalities, see Capturing and Managing Images for the Album in the  Infrastructure User's Guide.
