Relations

Relations located at a product level are not integrated to the product update process. To update them, Click the Asynchronous Relations Update icon ( ) or right-click the relation and select Asynchronous Relations Update. If your relation drives parameters located at a Part level, create the relation at the Part level.

 

Click the Add Set of Relations icon to regroup relations into categories.
Click the Asynchronous Relations Update icon to perform the update of asynchronous relations.
